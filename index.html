Bloggy

Create your own blog and share your thoughts with the world!

This web app is a blogging platform that lets you write and format your own posts with an advanced text editor (CKeditor). You can also personalize each page with a different header. The app is developed using Python Flask, SQLAlchemy, Relational Database, and WTFforms. The app also features authentication and hash encrypted secure password for the admin and the users. Users can sign up and comment on the posts, and they get a random profile picture.

Your Personal task Manager. Organize your life wuth custome To-Do Stickers
